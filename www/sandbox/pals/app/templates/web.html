A holiday celebrating the fraternal spirit of awesome pals!

With the quickening pace of modern society and the competing demands of family and work, men everywhere are organizing fewer poker nights, skipping midweek happy hours, and failing to watch important sporting events with their friends and neighbors.


Aren't the sacred bonds of friendships just as important as romantic love? Don't men deserve one day a year that we can shirk all responsibilities and sneak away with our pals to go fishing, eat our collective weight in pizza while pwning n00bs on xbox live, or test the ballpark's limits of 'disorderly conduct' at a double-header?


We think we do. And if you agree, we invite you to sign the petition and join the movement to officially recognize August 14th as Palentine’s Day.
